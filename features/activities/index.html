Biking

Trail access • rider-friendly base

Ride through paradise

Adventure

Based near Niebli de la Compania, with routes around the Pululahua area and Guayllabamba River. Stop in to refuel, tune up, and reset - or stay overnight to keep the adventure going.

Best for: mountain bikers Terrain: scenic + varied Extras: rest + refuel

Biker-friendly setup

  • Trail access points and route guidance (ask us)
  • Rest stop: drinks, snacks, and farm-to-table meals
  • Bike maintenance station: tools and space for quick fixes
  • Overnight stays: secure storage + a calm reset

Packages (example)

  • Day rider pass: trail access + rest stop amenities (inquire)
  • Overnight stay: lodging + dinner + breakfast + storage (inquire)
  • Groups/events: guided rides and catered options (custom)
