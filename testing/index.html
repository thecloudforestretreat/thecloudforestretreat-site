About Us

Escape to The Cloud Forest Retreat, a quiet eco-lodge in the heart of Mindo where sustainability meets serenity. Come for the views, stay for the calm, and leave feeling restored.

At a glance

Nestled in the Chocó Andino de Pichincha (a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve region), our retreat is built for slow mornings, fresh air, and the kind of stillness you only find in the cloud forest.

Our story

How this place came to life

The Cloud Forest Retreat was born from a deep love for nature and a simple idea: create a sanctuary where guests can experience the magic of the cloud forest without giving up comfort.

Here, mist moves through the valley, birds call from the canopy, and every view invites you to slow down. Whether you arrive for a weekend getaway, a solo reset, a trip with friends, or a family adventure, the forest meets you where you are.

"Breathe in the forest. Let the noise fade. Feel the day become yours again."

We built this retreat for people who want to reconnect: with nature, with loved ones, and with themselves.

Our commitment

Sustainability and conservation

Designed to protect what you came to see

We are committed to sustainability and preserving the natural beauty of the cloud forest. As part of the Chocó Andino de Pichincha region, we take pride in protecting a globally significant ecosystem.

Our practices focus on minimizing impact and supporting a healthier future for the forest and the communities connected to it.

How we reduce our footprint

  • Eco-minded design choices across the property
  • Energy and resource awareness (including solar where applicable)
  • Water conservation practices
  • Local sourcing and responsible stewardship
Focus: low impact Goal: preserve biodiversity Approach: thoughtful hosting
