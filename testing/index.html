Ingredients are picked at peak ripeness and used fresh. Meals here reflect what the land offers in that moment, creating a direct connection between soil, season, and flavor.

  • Harvest in the morning for peak freshness.
  • Cook simple meals that let ingredients shine.
  • Rotate menus based on what is ready that week.

What you might see in season

  • Tree tomatoes, citrus, and seasonal fruits.
  • Peppers, leafy greens, herbs, and garden harvests.
  • Coffee cherries when conditions are right.
