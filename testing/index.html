How to Get to Cloud Forest Retreat from Quito

Getting here is less about complicated logistics and more about understanding the journey. It is a gradual shift from city energy into something quieter, greener, and slower.

Check Availability Ask a Question

Direct answer

The retreat is reached from Quito by road, and the trip usually takes a few hours depending on your starting point and timing. Most travelers plan it as part of a short nature escape, allowing enough time to arrive without rushing and settle in comfortably.

What the journey actually feels like

The route out of Quito slowly changes as you leave the city behind. Traffic fades, the air becomes cooler, and the scenery shifts toward mountains and dense greenery. It does not feel like a long-distance trip, but it does feel like a clear transition.

By the time you arrive, the pace is already different. That is why many travelers find the journey itself helps them disconnect before the stay even begins.

The setting is part of the reason to come

The cloud forest environment is very different from the city. The road helps you transition into that atmosphere naturally.

How the arrival shapes the stay

A calm arrival makes everything easier. You settle in faster, enjoy the surroundings more, and start the experience without stress.

Most travelers who plan the trip with a bit of margin find that the transition from Quito to the cloud forest becomes one of the most memorable parts of the visit.
